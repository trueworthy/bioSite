Hobbies

Motorcycles & Riding

One of my biggest passions is motorcycles, both riding them and working on them. There’s something freeing about hitting the open road, feeling the rumble of the engine beneath you, and just enjoying the ride. I also love working on bikes, customizing them, and making them my own. The picture I chose is from a ride I took with a buddy, a great day spent on the road doing something I love.

Traveling

Traveling has always been one of my favorite hobbies, especially when I get to share the experience with my wife. Last year, we went to Disneyland for the first time, and it was an unforgettable adventure. Exploring new places together creates lasting memories, and I’m looking forward to many more trips with her as we discover more of the world.

Video Games

Video games have always been a great way for me to unwind, and Helldivers 2 has quickly become one of my favorite games to play. It’s a perfect mix of strategy, teamwork, and action, and I love diving into the game with friends or on my own. Gaming is not just a hobby, it’s a fun escape where I can challenge myself and enjoy some downtime.
