About

“You can't take the sky from me.” ― Joss Whedon

Jacob A Haselton was born in Denver, Colorado. He is the youngest child out of five, three boys and two girls. Jacob moved around frequently but called Fallon, Nevada his home. At the age of 5, Jacob lost one of his gorgeous eyes to a screwdriver being thrown at him. Due to this event, he now can not see out of his right eye. Fortunately, he still has a normal life, but he always knows what he is going to be for halloween, a pirate. While in high school, you would find Jake with his 5 best friends messing around. They got into lots of trouble pulling pranks, flipping cars, riding bikes, and causing mischief. During his wild child years, Jacob started working on motorcycles. This gave him a passion and drive for motorcycles and mechanics.

After graduating Churchill County High School in 2013, Jacob moved to Reno, Nevada to attend the University of Nevada, Reno also known as UNR. When he’s not tinkering with his many motorcycles, he is studying to become a Mechanical Engineer. In the pursuit of a higher education, Jacob met his fabulous girlfriend, Lea (which so happens to be me) on a chilly March night. They have been together for almost 4 years and live happily with their 8 pets, 4 cats and 4 reptiles. Jacob hopes to graduate with his Mechanical Engineering degree. Later in life, he hopes to open a custom motorcycle shop where he can design and build unique, one of a kind motorcycles.
